The Love That Dies Within Me!!

I Know Marraige Isn't meant for us

But may be love is.

I know its complicated

But I Love You.

May be it's needs to die within me

to never reach you

to never make you feel it.

These thousands emotions I carry for you

not a single one ever reaches you.

It will die within me

To Feel his warm hugs,

I lost you when you held me in your arms,

only to push me into the reality that ssaid you were never mine to lose...!!!
